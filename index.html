Motorcycles & Scooters

Motorcycles and scooters are two-wheeled vehicles that are powered by an engine. Motorcycles typically have larger engines and are designed for speed and power, while scooters are smaller and more fuel-efficient, and are often used for urban transportation.

2021 Honda CBR500R

Honda CBR500R is a sport-oriented motorcycle in the Honda CBR series. It has a liquid-cooled, parallel-twin engine with a displacement of 471cc and produces 47 horsepower and 32 lb-ft of torque. It has a six-speed transmission, and a chain final drive.
